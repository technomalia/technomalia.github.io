About this Interview

For this activity, Joanne volunteered herself to interview a work colleague she already works with so after a quick discussion we came to an agreement on a list of questions for her to ask after which she arranged lunch with co-worker - Rael - then met up and conducted the interview on everyone's behalf.

This page contains both the audio and rather large transcription of Joanne's interview - the write-up can be found in our submission PDF:
