SmartPet-20 Animal Translation Collar

The SmartPet-20 is an innovation in animal husbandry that closes the gap like no other device on the market, between human and animal.

Using advanced analog and digital physical sensors plugged into ground-breaking Artificial Intelligence our collar can tell you how your animal is feeling at any given moment - in human speech.

That might sound too good to be true, but it's really not.
