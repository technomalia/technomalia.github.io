The Product

The SmartPet-20 Animal Translation Device

The Interview Return Home Team Profiling

A giant leap for petcare..

Imagine no longer having to guess how your pets, livestock or other animals are feeling and how their general health/well-being is affecting them on any given day only to have them respond with that blank stare and wagging tail or purring that gives nothing away but how happy they are to see you?

It's long been known that animals are naturally good at hiding any illness they may have out of fear of being cast out of their social group or pack and we will rarely see a pet behave in a way that betrays any negative impact on their health until that animal is so critically ill it can no longer act and by this point it's usually too late to do anything about it.

The SmartPet-20 is an innovation in animal husbandry bringing animals and humans closer than ever before fixing the communication gap using revolutionary new technology that gives owners knowledge and detailed information about their animals through a combination of hardware sensors that feed into an advanced AI core that acts just like a tiny brain - analyzing your animals physical and emotional states in real-time and reporting back to you in real human speech.

How does it work?

The SmartPet-20 might sound like just another fitbit pinned onto a pet collar, but there are a plethora of additional hardware and software implimentations that make our technology so different and so intelligent it makes other smart devices look like little more than pocket-calculators.

The most up to date sensors many of us wear on our wrists every day to monitor pulse, step-count and stress levels was just the ground-floor in creating our translator: we have taken the standard hardware included in most market-leading smart-watches today then build on that with the inclusion of the follow features to name just a few:

  • Body-language Tracking
    Our patented micro-gyroscope monitors your animals direct movement in real-time to determine behaviours leading to stance, tilt, position which - when combined with an advanced glonass GPS chipset - keeps track of animal orientation, movement speed and this hardware combination is responsible for most of the heavy-lifting in regard to tracking your pets body-language.
  • Health Monitoring
    Combined in parralell with the continual body-language tracking is the simultaneous monitoring of heart-rate and levels of stress as well as oxygen content through a compound photoplethysmographic sensor that reads all your pets vital signs while only requiring 1mm of surface contact against the skin.
  • Advanced Artificial-Intelligence Core
    Both the previously described sensors feed into an onboard AI system that reads-in all available data from your pet and compares current behaviour with a history the software maintains for each individual animal and using lodicrously complex conditional logic, determines where in the spectrum of mood, feeling and health your animal is at that current point in time - it takes our device only 0.06 seconds to determine this result.

Customizable modules & opensource API

The SmartPet-20 is not only designed for pets or dog and cats by any means, with not only different collars, harnesses and straps to fit most animals but a system of modules that can be easily downloaded directly to the device via wifi that's read-in by the AI core: want units to fit your horses? just select the horse collar and plugin module - the device will instantly know exactly the range of health attributes, dimensions and body-language patterns horses present.
